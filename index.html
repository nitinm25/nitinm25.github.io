About

I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Vijay Chidambaram.

Previously, I was part of the Complex Analytics & Scalable Processing Systems Lab (CASP Lab) at Boston University, where I was advised by Prof. John Liagouris. My research there focused on building scalable systems for Multi-Party Computation (MPC).
